The Economy of Death

A poetic data visualization project exploring
mortality and meaning across America.

This project uses data from the CDC’s Underlying Cause of Death dataset (1999–2019), presenting the age-adjusted death rates for the 10 leading causes of death across U.S. states.

Data are based on all resident death certificates filed in the 50 states and D.C., classified by the International Classification of Diseases, Tenth Revision (ICD–10). Death rates are per 100,000 population, standardized to the 2000 U.S. population.

Sources: CDC/NCHS, National Vital Statistics System, and CDC WONDER.
www.cdc.gov/nchs/deaths.htm   |   wonder.cdc.gov
