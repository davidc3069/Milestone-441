And on our journey to this dystopian heaven,
does a son get to stay tethered to the machine
that breathes for him?

Three times a week.
The repetition, the rhythm, the ritual of survival.
Consistency is key. Consistency is life.

A donor is turned away.
A kidney is lost to the highest bidder.
But the numbers remain steady, fret not.

Tomorrow, the ledger will not be noticed.

1999
